✅ Key Takeaways When a book is missing from the shelf: (1) Offer a shelf trace immediately, (2) check for an e-book or digital version, (3) initiate an ILL rush request, (4) stay calm and solution-focused — never make the patron feel dismissed.

🔑 The Golden Protocol for Missing Items 1. Apologize sincerely for the system discrepancy.
2. Immediately initiate a shelf trace — check nearby shelves, returns cart, and hold shelf.
3. Offer a digital/e-book alternative while the trace is underway.
4. If unavailable digitally, initiate an ILL rush request.
5. Never blame the patron, the system, or colleagues.
